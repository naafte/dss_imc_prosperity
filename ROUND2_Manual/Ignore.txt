# Thoughts & Ideas

## General

- Research should be around 13–23% (target ~15%). Always good to increase Scale.
- Cannot expect players to act rationally.
- Might be some random players who will do 100% or 0% just for fun, so we will likely never be top or bottom for any of the three pillars.

## Speed Strategy

Gemini and Claude have been pushing me to fight for speed at 35–50% and 50–70% respectively. Expect **many** players to use AI and listen to this.

- If 90% of players fight for ~50% and we go 49, we're gonna get bottom 10% speed — so we might have to fight for this.
- Many players might also expect many people to fight for this and take the back seat. If 50% fight and 50% take a back seat, we can try winning the bottom 50%?

## Full Arms Race Scenarios

**What Claude says:**
> Full arms race: Everyone panics into speed. Mode near v=45–50. Widespread misallocation — being at v=0 here earns a ~0.3 multiplier but the best absolute PnL given what others waste. Mean speed ≈ 46%.

- Basically we should invest very little in speed.

**What Gemini says:**
> Betting 0% on speed and getting 0.1 is significantly worse than betting 50% and getting 0.9. Scenario B absolutely crushes Scenario A, even though Scenario B sacrificed half their base stats to get there. The difference between a 0.1x multiplier and a 0.9x multiplier is mathematically a 900% difference, whereas stripping 50% out of your Scale/Research budget only reduces your base output by about 60%.

- We should not invest 0%?
